Arduino Project Model

About this project

This page is an interactive model of a device I created in real life using an arduino board. On the board, I attached an LED bulb, two buttons, and a buzzer, and then connected them all correctly using wires. The way it works is that there are two buttons, which I will refer to as button 1 (top) and button 2 (bottom). When you press and hold button 1, the LED will light up. When you press button two, nothing happens. However, when you press and hold button 1 and button 2, the light will light up and the buzzer will play a sound.

How I modeled this on my website was a little different. For the actual device to work, you need to press and hold both buttons at the same time, but due to the fact that there is no way to click and hold both buttons simultaneously, so I had to come up with a compromise. On this model, you can toggle button 1 on and off by clicking it. Button two can be activated by clicking and holding it, just as you would on the actual device.
